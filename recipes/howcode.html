how to learn code

  • at about 10 years of age, you:
    • ask strangers on the neopets forums to code your neohomepage for you
    • a kind stranger will actually do this for you
    • you will spend many months randomly changing things in the code until you gain a very rudimentary understanding of what that does
  • now that you can change the color of a link you consider yourself highly skilled
  • promptly forget everything after the neopets phase ends
  • eventually you create a tumblr blog and try to do the same thing you did on neopets
  • it does not work
  • remain satisfied with editing redux
  • promptly forget everything once you switch to mobile blogging
  • fast forward many years: you purchase a personal url
  • you link that to a neocities site
  • you realize you are getting nowhere with w3schools
  • friend recommends odin project!
  • LOOK AT ME NOW!!!!
