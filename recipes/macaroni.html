grandma's macaroni

Your finished product will look similar to picture above:

  1. Gather ingredients:
    1. Angel hair pasta
    2. Block of Velveeta cheese
    3. Milk
    4. Salt
  2. Boil pot of water
  3. Add angel hair pasta when boiling
  4. Strain when perfectly al dente
    • Test perfection of pasta by throwing a noodle on the wall behind the stove!
    • When it sticks to the wall, it is perfect
  5. Strain pasta
  6. Add at least half of the block of Velveeta cheese
  7. Add splash of milk
    • As Velveeta melts, add milk until desired cheese sauce texture is achieved
  8. Add salt to taste
  9. Bon appetit!
